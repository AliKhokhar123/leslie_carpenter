About Our Team

Mitzi and Karen are our Office Administrator and Office Assistant, respectively. Both of them have backgrounds as Registered Dental Assistants giving them a strong clinical foundation that they bring to assist patients and staff with everything from first visit through follow-up, working to schedule with specialists, and understanding your account and any insurance benefits.

Sophia and Julie are our Clinical and Administrative Assistants and share training in chairside assisting. Sophia will be at your side for procedures and coordinates lab cases as well as keeping supplies available and instruments sterilized. Julie works to keep incoming communication from specialists up to date in patient files as well as managing safety protocols for the office.

Pam, Erin, and McKenna are our experienced and capable dental hygienists and together offer knowledgeable, skillful, experienced, and thorough care to enable our patients’ best periodontal health. They work along with Dr Carpenter to assess and then determine correct care for each person’s condition and circumstances.
